Welcome to the DHHS Program

This is a new program established by the state and federal government to improve and create a significant impact in the country's economy. The following categories of people are eligible to apply:

  • Old
  • Young (16+)
  • Full workers
  • Part-time workers
  • Unemployed
  • Retired
  • Divorced
  • Married
  • Single
  • Business owners
  • Widow or widower

Difference Between DHHS and Loans

DHHS Loan
No need of higher credit score Higher credit score needed
No need to pay it back because it's free Need to pay it back with interest
You can get the money anytime and anywhere You need proof of resident
